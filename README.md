Cold email generator for  company using groq, langchain and streamlit. It allows users to input the URL of a company's careers page. The tool then extracts job listings from that page and generates personalized cold emails. These emails include relevant portfolio links sourced from a vector database, based on the specific job description.
Imagine a scenario where Tesla is looking for a Senior Data Scientist to enhance their AI and machine learning capabilities for self-driving cars. Tesla is investing significant time and resources into the hiring process, onboarding, and training new employees.
DataX, a leading data science consultancy, can provide a dedicated Senior Data Scientist who is highly skilled in machine learning algorithms, data analysis, and AI modeling, making them an excellent fit for Tesla’s needs. The business development executive (Ava) at DataX is reaching out to Tesla via a cold email to offer their expertise
